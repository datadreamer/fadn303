Pseudo-classes


Pseudo-classes are ways to define styles that apply to elements under specific conditions.

Link States


a:visited

The condition of the link when the targeted web site has already been visited by the client.
a:hover

The condition of the link when the clients cursor hovers over the link area.
a:active

The condition of the link when it has been selected (clicked on) by a client.
